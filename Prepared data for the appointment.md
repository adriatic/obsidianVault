### Vehicle Registration (Out-of-State or New to CA)

- Completed Application for Title or Registration (REG 343 form).[](https://frontdoor.ucdavis.edu/relocation/car)
    
- Out-of-state title (or current title from the prior owner, if applicable).[](https://frontdoor.ucdavis.edu/relocation/car)
    
- Out-of-state vehicle registration (if the vehicle is used and previously registered).[](https://frontdoor.ucdavis.edu/relocation/car)
    
- Proof of insurance meeting California minimum requirements.[](https://www.dmv.ca.gov/portal/driver-licenses-identification-cards/instruction-permits/)
    
- Valid smog certificate (if applicable, usually for gasoline vehicles >4 years old).[](https://frontdoor.ucdavis.edu/relocation/car)
    
- Payment for registration and title fees.[](https://frontdoor.ucdavis.edu/relocation/car)
    
- Vehicle for on-site VIN verification (required for out-of-state vehicles).[](https://frontdoor.ucdavis.edu/relocation/car)
    

### Driver’s License (NEW, Transfer, or Renewal)

- Proof of identity (valid passport, birth certificate, or other approved document).[](https://www.dmv.ca.gov/portal/driver-education-and-safety/special-interest-driver-guides/new-to-california/)
    
- Social Security Card or official proof of Social Security number.[](https://keycloak.keenethics.com/blog/california-dmv-appointment-a-step)
    
- Two documents verifying California residency (utility bill, lease, mortgage statement, etc.).[](https://www.dmv.ca.gov/portal/driver-licenses-identification-cards/real-id/real-id-checklist/)
    
- Current driver’s license (if transferring from out-of-state).[](https://keycloak.keenethics.com/blog/california-dmv-appointment-a-step)
    
- Proof of legal presence in the U.S. if not already established.[](https://www.dmv.ca.gov/portal/driver-licenses-identification-cards/instruction-permits/)
    
- Application fee for a driver’s license.[](https://www.dmv.ca.gov/portal/driver-licenses-identification-cards/instruction-permits/)
    

## Optional/Specific to Appointment

- Appointment confirmation number.[](https://ranchosmogcenter.com/california-dmv-online-appointment-guide/)
    


## For Vehicle Registration (New to CA or Out-of-State Vehicle)

- Completed Application for Title or Registration (Form REG 343)
    
- Out-of-state title
    
- Out-of-state registration
    
- Proof of insurance
    
- Valid smog certificate (if your vehicle requires it)
    
- Payment for fees
    
- The vehicle itself, for a VIN inspection on-site[](https://frontdoor.ucdavis.edu/relocation/car)
    

## For Driver’s License (New, Transfer, or Renewal)

- Proof of identity (passport, birth certificate, etc.)
    
- Social Security card or proof of Social Security number
    
- Two documents showing proof of California residency (e.g., utility bill, lease agreement)
    
- Current driver’s license (if transferring from another state)
    
- Payment for license fee[](https://www.dmv.ca.gov/portal/driver-education-and-safety/special-interest-driver-guides/new-to-california/)
    

## For REAL ID (if applying)

- Identity document (e.g., passport or certified birth certificate)
    
- Social Security number proof
    
- Two proofs of California residency[](https://www.dmv.ca.gov/portal/driver-licenses-identification-cards/real-id/real-id-checklist/)
    

Always bring original documents or certified copies as required and check if your appointment includes Real ID requirements, which are stricter. Also, bring your appointment confirmation number.[](https://ranchosmogcenter.com/california-dmv-online-appointment-guide/)
